About LeeCyberSec

LeeCyberSec was founded in 2016 and is an awards winning provider of cyber security, compliance, infrastructure and incident response services to organizations in the United States, Canada, Asia Pacific and further afield. LeeCyberSec also delivers services across the US, UK, Europe, Africa and the Middle East from its EMEA headquarters in Baltimore, Maryland, USA.

LeeCyberSec provides true global coverage, allowing it to respond to the intricate regulatory nuances required in today’s interconnected environment. With every engagement we aim to provide tailored and pragmatic consultancy services that are designed to meet the client’s unique challenges.

Accreditations

We are proud to be one of a handful of companies worldwide that are certified by CREST, CESG CHECK and CESG CLAS. LeeCyberSec is also a PCI ASV, PCI QSA, P2PE QSA, PA QSA and ISO 27001 lead auditor. LeeCyberSec is also an accredited supplier of CBEST testing services and an approved provider of STAR testing services. Our highly regarded accreditations are underpinned by our rigorous quality practices and procedures, which are demonstrated throughout LeeCyberSec.

We offer strong security services to our clients such as penetration testing and the full spectrum of PCI DSS. However, we also practise strong security procedures from within our own organisation through our commitment to ISO 27001 and ISO 9001. LeeCyberSec is also an ‘Investor In People’ company, with strong environmental and sustainability policies through ISO 14001.

Our Clients

At LeeCyberSec we are passionate about information security and managing cyber risk – keeping your data and your business secure and protected at every stage of its journey. Our aim is to be ‘your trusted cyber partner’. LeeCyberSec believes that a holistic approach to security is needed, whatever industry you work in.

LeeCyberSec has been delivering security assessment services for financial service firms across the world for more than a decade. As a CREST company, we have certified infrastructure, application and wireless testers as well as a team of consultants that have been active contributors to the STAR and the CBEST programs.

We aim to impart knowledge, advice and assistance to help you deploy changes in behaviour, understanding, and where appropriate, culture. This will then provide a robust foundation, which will help you develop and grow the company’s security platform. This methodology has been at the core of our services, helping provide pragmatic cyber security for industries such as IT, Technology and Engineering.
